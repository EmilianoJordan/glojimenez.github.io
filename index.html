About

I'm a climate scientist interested in finding innovative ways to solve environmental problems. I'm pursuing my Ph.D. in Geosciences at the University of Arizona, working jointly with Julia Cole and Kevin Anchukaitis. My research aims to understand how the Pacific Ocean and El Niño will respond to climate change. My current projects include using corals from the Galápagos Islands to reconstruct the recent history of the Eastern Pacific Ocean and determining ENSO states via classification techniques. When I take off my scientist hat, I enjoy collecting athletic hobbies and recipes.
